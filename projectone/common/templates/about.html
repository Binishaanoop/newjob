About Us

DreaM JoB is a global leader in connecting people and jobs. Every day, DreaM JoB aims to make every workplace happier and more productive by transforming the way employers and candidates find the right fit. For 25 years, DreaM JoB has worked to transform the recruiting industry. Today, the company leverages advanced technology using intelligent digital, social and mobile solutions, including the flagship website DreaM JoB.com, DreaM JoB’s innovative app, and a vast array of products and services.

At DreaM JoB, our mission is to help people get jobs. We have more than 11,500 global employees passionately pursuing this purpose and improving the recruitment journey through real stories and data. We foster a collaborative workplace that strives to create the best experience for job seekers.
